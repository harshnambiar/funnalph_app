What is Funnalph?
Funnalph is a Click Funnel style project on the Alephium Network. It allows users to create ALPH-based click funnels, known as "funnalphs."
What is the purpose of Funnalph?
Funnalph enables ecosystem participants like developers, influencers, and artists to raise funds and resources by creating their own funnels. Agents can also earn commissions by promoting these funnels.
What is in it for the ecosystem?
Funnalph bridges the gap between builders (projects, developers, artists) and marketers (influencers, community members) to foster synergy within the Alephium ecosystem.
How does it work?
Currently invite-only for projects. Fill the form or email us at thedarksunlabs@gmail.com with your project details, and we'll get back within 7 business days.
What does it cost to create a funnel for my project?
Absolutely nothing! Funnalph is free and will always be free.
Which wallets are supported?
Currently supports the Alephium Wallet Extension on Chrome/Chromium. More wallets will be supported as we progress to Mainnet.
How long will my Funnel be active?
Funnels remain active indefinitely but inactive ones may be buried under the active ones in the feed.
Can I have multiple funnels?
Only one funnel per wallet is allowed to discourage monopolization and centralization.
How are the funds distributed?
Projects receive 90%, agents earn 9%, and 1% goes to the platform as a fee.
Who can become an Agent?
Anyone can become an Agent, whether they have a large following or none at all.
Where do I sign up as a project or agent?
Click the "Apply" button on the Funnels page to get started.
